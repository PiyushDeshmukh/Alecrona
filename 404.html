Do not go where the path may lead, go instead where there is no path and leave a trail


You will either find a way, or make one

Any path is only a path, and there is no affront, to oneself or to others, in dropping it if that is what your heart tells you...Look at every path closely and deliberately. Try it as many times as you think necessary. Then ask yourself, and yourself alone, one question.. Does this path have a way? If it does, then go ahead; if it doesn't you spoiled the surprise
